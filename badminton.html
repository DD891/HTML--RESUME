• Badminton was first played in 1873 in England.
  • The game is also rooted in China, Greece, and India.
  • The Duke introduced the game to his guests at a lawn-party held at his estate in Gloucestershire.
  • The Duke called it "the Badminton game" after the name of his estate.
  • Badminton had its Olympic debut in Barcelona in 1992.
  • Badminton was introduced to the Commonwealth Games in Kingston Jamaica in 1966.
